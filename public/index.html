Sleep Hygiene Tips
⏰ Consistency
Same bed and wake time every day, even weekends.
📵 Screen-free wind down
Stop screens 60 min before bed. Blue light suppresses melatonin.
🌡 Cool room
Ideal sleep temp is 65–68°F. Core temp must drop to initiate sleep.
☕ Caffeine cutoff
Stop caffeine 6–8 hours before bed. Half-life is ~5 hours.
🌙 Dark environment
Blackout curtains or a sleep mask. Even dim light disrupts quality.
🧘 Wind-down ritual
Light stretching or reading 30 min before bed signals your brain to slow down.
